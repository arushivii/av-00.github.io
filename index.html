ABOUT JODC

JIIT Month of Code is an online programme by the open-source club of JIIT NOIDA, the JODC, focused on introducing students to open source software development and documentation writing.
Students will collaborate and contribute to open-source projects under the guidance of mentor(s) for 3 weeks during the winter.
